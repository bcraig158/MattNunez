Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What is your typical response time?

We typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent requests, we recommend using WhatsApp or Signal for immediate assistance.

Do you offer 24/7 support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency support for existing clients. New inquiries are handled during business hours for the best service quality.

What information should I provide in my initial inquiry?

Please include your preferred dates, number of guests, destination, and any specific requirements or preferences. The more detail you provide, the better we can tailor our proposal.

How do you ensure privacy and discretion?

We take privacy extremely seriously. All communications are confidential, and we use secure channels like Signal for sensitive information. We never share client details without explicit permission.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, wire transfers, and cryptocurrency. Payment terms are typically 50% deposit to confirm, with the balance due 30 days prior to travel.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for larger groups and extended stays. Contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements and receive a customized quote.
