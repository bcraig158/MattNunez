Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury yacht services and experiences.

What's included in a luxury yacht charter?

Our luxury yacht charters include professional crew, gourmet catering, premium beverages, water sports equipment, and personalized service. We handle all the details from itinerary planning to onboard entertainment.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for peak season (June-August, December-January). For off-peak seasons, 1-2 months notice is typically sufficient. Last-minute bookings may be available subject to yacht availability.

What destinations do you offer?

We offer charters worldwide including the Mediterranean, Caribbean, French Riviera, Dubai, and custom destinations. Our network spans over 50 exclusive locations, and we can arrange charters in any destination you desire.

How many guests can your yachts accommodate?

Our fleet ranges from intimate 6-guest vessels to large 20-guest luxury yachts. We can also arrange multiple yachts for larger groups. Each yacht is carefully selected to ensure comfort and luxury for your specific group size.

What's the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 90+ days before departure receive a full refund. 60-89 days: 75% refund. 30-59 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: no refund. We recommend travel insurance for additional protection.

Do you provide crew and staff?

Yes, all our charters include a professional crew including captain, chef, stewardess, and deckhands. Additional staff such as masseuses, instructors, or entertainers can be arranged upon request.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our chefs can accommodate any dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, kosher, halal, and other special diets. We'll discuss your preferences during the planning process.

What activities are available onboard?

Activities include water sports (jet skis, paddleboards, snorkeling), fishing, spa treatments, yoga sessions, fine dining, entertainment, and cultural excursions. We can arrange any specific activities you desire.

How do I get to the yacht?

We handle all transportation arrangements including airport transfers, helicopter charters, and private car services. Our concierge team will coordinate your entire journey from arrival to departure.

What's the booking process?

1. Contact us with your requirements
2. We'll create a personalized proposal
3. Review and confirm details
4. Secure booking with deposit
5. Final planning and coordination
6. Enjoy your luxury experience
